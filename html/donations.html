Support ITKAN Robotics

Every Dollar Fuels a Future in STEM for our Muslim Community

Your contributions help students at ITKAN Robotics gain hands-on experience with STEM. Whether it's a $5 donation for small tools or a $500+ contribution for advanced equipment, every bit counts and directly benefits our program and students.

Supporter
$5 - $50

Help us purchase microcontrollers, wires, batteries, and other small tools essential to our weekly projects.

Enabler
$75 - $150

Assist us in acquiring advanced kits, sensors, and components for intermediate robotics builds.

Champion
$200 - $500

Provide full kits or fund a full season of materials for a team of students to prepare for competitions.

Visionary
$500+

Help us invest in large-scale equipment like 3D printers or laser cutters to dramatically expand our project capabilities.

Want to donate? Contact us directly or drop off your donation at Palos Islamic Center during open hours.
