ITKAN Robotics at PIC

Building Community Through STEM

Season Highlights

Despite starting our robotics program late into the season, our passionate staff and determined students pulled together and placed Top 8 in the State!

We proudly fielded two full teams — including an all-girls team who earned the CONNECT Award for outstanding teamwork, community involvement, and perseverance.

Our success was made possible by grassroots support: we organized multiple bake sales, and thanks to effective outreach and marketing, we were sponsored by dozens of local businesses. This support helped us fund equipment, travel, and competition fees.
